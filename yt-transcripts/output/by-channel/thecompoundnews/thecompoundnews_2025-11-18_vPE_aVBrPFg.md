# They Just Invented the Agentic AI Brokerage

**Channel:** The Compound
**Published:** 2025-11-18
**URL:** https://youtube.com/watch?v=vPE_aVBrPFg
**Duration:** 34:04

## Summary

This episode features a live demo by Public co-founders Leif Abraham and Jannick Malling of two new AI-powered brokerage features launched in November 2025. Generated Assets enables retail investors to create a custom index using a natural language prompt, with the platform's multi-agent system evaluating stocks, assigning relevancy scores, equal- or custom-weighting positions, and producing a backtest versus the S&P 500. The Agentic Brokerage, announced for Q1 2026 rollout, takes portfolio instructions in natural language and executes them autonomously, effectively replicating a wealth management service layer for self-directed investors.

## Key Takeaways

- Generated Assets lets any user create a custom investable index by typing a thesis prompt, removing the need for spreadsheets or manual ticker research.
- The AI behind Generated Assets dispatches thousands of sub-agents to score each stock for relevancy against the user's stated criteria, including qualitative factors sourced from news and analyst reports.
- The Agentic Brokerage can handle complex multi-condition instructions such as conditional sells triggered by Fed rate decisions or automated cash sweeps between accounts.
- Public's fractional trading engine, launched in 2019, is positioned as the essential infrastructure layer that makes investing small amounts across a 200-stock custom index feasible.
- Generated Assets supports thematic, fundamental, qualitative, and ETF-remixing use cases, covering scenarios where no existing ETF precisely matches an investor's thesis.
- The platform draws a parallel between the current agentic AI shift and prior interface revolutions from phone to web to mobile, framing natural language as the next UI layer.
- A social sharing feature for generated assets was launched alongside the product, enabling community portfolio discovery and performance transparency.

## Detailed Notes

### Product Launch Overview [00:01:16]

Public announced three AI-related initiatives simultaneously: AI research tools (in development for years), Generated Assets (launched fully that day), and the Agentic Brokerage (announced with a Q1 2026 waitlist). Generated Assets was previously available in preview form at generatedassets.com since May 2025 without an investment capability.

### Generated Assets: How It Works [00:02:34]

Users enter a natural language prompt describing an investment thesis. A master agent interprets the intent and dispatches evaluation agents to score all public market stocks for relevancy. Results are weighted by relevancy score, market cap, or equal weight at user discretion, and the index is auto-backtested against the S&P 500. An asset score above 1.0 indicates risk-adjusted outperformance of the S&P Sharpe ratio.

### Live Demo: Low RPU / High User Base Index [00:22:16]

The hosts prompted an index of companies with large user bases but low average revenue per user, surfacing 25 names including PayPal, Spotify, Pinterest, Zillow, and eBay. Each position includes a written AI rationale; PayPal's relevancy was 100% with stated $73 annual revenue per user across 430 million active accounts. The backtest showed the index significantly outperforming the S&P 500.

### Live Demo: Gen Z Consumer Habits Index [00:27:13]

A second prompt targeting companies benefiting from Gen Z consumer habits surfaced Spotify, Pinterest, Airbnb, Shopify, Live Nation, Netflix, Uber, and Starbucks (lowest relevancy at 75/100). Sony appeared due to PlayStation's relevance to Gen Z gaming. The demo illustrated how low-relevancy inclusions are explained and how users can refine or exclude them.

### Agentic Brokerage and Future Roadmap [00:03:06]

The Agentic Brokerage is designed to execute multi-condition portfolio instructions autonomously, such as tax-aware trim schedules, conditional rebalancing tied to macro events, and automated cash management. Future versions of Generated Assets are planned to include options, crypto, and bonds to support structured product creation. The founders describe the agent-plus-generated-asset combination as enabling a fully personalized tactical investment methodology.
