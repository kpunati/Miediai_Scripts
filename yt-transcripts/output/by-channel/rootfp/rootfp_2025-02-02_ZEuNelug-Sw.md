# Why We Just Launched A NEW Retirement Community: Root Collective

**Channel:** James Conole, CFP®
**Published:** 2025-02-02
**URL:** https://youtube.com/watch?v=ZEuNelug-Sw
**Duration:** 14:56

## Summary

James Conole and Ari Taublieb announce the launch of Root Collective, a new online community aimed at people approaching or already in retirement. The community is designed to go beyond financial advice to address the social, emotional, and lifestyle dimensions of retirement that traditional advisory services do not cover. Key features include peer discussion, expert guests on topics like health, travel, and finance, live interactive shows, and direct access to Root advisors in a community format. Pricing starts at $50 per month with a $20 discounted founding member rate, and hardship accommodations are offered.

## Key Takeaways

- Root Collective is designed to democratize access to high-quality retirement education and community beyond the firm's one-on-one client base.
- The community addresses non-financial retirement challenges including purpose, community, health, and travel that traditional advisory firms do not cover.
- A peer community provides validation and experiential wisdom that advisors—who are not themselves retirees—cannot fully offer.
- Expert guests on health, travel, insurance, and finance will complement peer discussions to create a broad retirement resource hub.
- Pricing is set deliberately low at $20/month for early members and financial hardship accommodations are explicitly available.
- The community will evolve based on member input, with the long-term vision of becoming a go-to daily resource for retirement planning and lifestyle.

## Detailed Notes

### Rationale for Launching Root Collective [00:00:00]

The founders identified that even well-funded retirees can experience anxiety that financial advice alone cannot resolve. Hearing from peers who have navigated similar fears provides a different kind of reassurance than advisor guidance. The community also addresses the reality that Root Financial cannot take every client, so the community extends impact beyond traditional advising.

### Community Features and Format [00:04:09]

Root Collective will host expert guests on topics spanning travel, health, insurance, and financial strategy. Scheduled shows and forums will allow real-time interaction rather than the asynchronous engagement typical of YouTube comment sections. Both James Conole and Ari Taublieb will be active participants, and the programming will adapt based on member preferences and feedback.

### Who It Is For and Pricing [00:06:14]

The community is open to anyone, with current Root Financial clients receiving free access. For non-clients, the price starts at $50 per month, with a $20 founding member rate locked in for early joiners regardless of future price changes. People with financial hardship can request discounted or free access by emailing hello@rootfinancial.com. The community is a single unified space merging both James Conole's and Ari Taublieb's audiences.

### Vision and Differentiation [00:11:27]

The presenters describe Root Collective as a prototype for what the future of financial advice could look like: holistic, community-driven, and accessible at various price points. It is not a substitute for one-on-one advisory relationships but a complementary resource for those who want education, community, and inspiration. The Retirement Planning Academy and Early Retirement Academy are separate products, with discounted cross-access available.
