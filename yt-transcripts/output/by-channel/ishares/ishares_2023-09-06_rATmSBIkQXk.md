## Summary

Jay Jacobs walks through how investors can access the AI megatrend via thematic ETFs. Highlights AI's reach across industries (energy efficiency, medicine, agriculture, content delivery) and projects $13 trillion in additional economic output by 2030 from AI users. Surveys three iShares thematic ETFs: IRBO (global robotics and AI), SOXX (US semiconductors), IDRV (autonomous vehicles).

## Key Takeaways

- AI is reshaping industries from energy and medicine to agriculture and content delivery — a comparable inflection to the internet.
- AI users are projected to generate $13 trillion in additional economic output by 2030.
- Three iShares ETFs offer different angles on AI exposure: IRBO (global robotics/AI), SOXX (US semiconductors), IDRV (autonomous vehicles).
- Thematic ETFs let investors capture a trend without picking the single winning company.
- Semiconductor exposure (SOXX) captures companies that supply AI infrastructure even if the AI user-company isn't directly held.

## Detailed Notes

### AI is reshaping daily life [00:00:01]

### Industry-level impact and economic scale [00:00:36]

AI is improving energy efficiency, developing new medicines, enhancing agricultural output, and delivering personalized content. Projects $13 trillion in additional economic output by 2030 from AI users.

### Three iShares ETFs for AI exposure [00:01:07]

IRBO holds global companies leading robotics and AI. SOXX targets US semiconductors — the foundational hardware layer. IDRV targets autonomous vehicles, an adjacent AI value chain. Together they offer angles for investors with different views on which part of the AI ecosystem will benefit most.
