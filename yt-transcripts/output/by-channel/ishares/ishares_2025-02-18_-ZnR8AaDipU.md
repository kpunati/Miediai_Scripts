## Summary

Karen Veraperry, head of US fixed income strategy at iShares, announces the launch of iShares' first two money market ETFs: the iShares Government Money Market ETF (primarily short-term government securities) and the iShares Prime Money Market ETF (broader money-market instruments including corporate commercial paper and CDs). Both are actively managed within strict regulatory guidelines and packaged as ETFs for trading flexibility.

## Key Takeaways

- iShares launched its first two money market ETFs in early 2025: a Government MM ETF and a Prime MM ETF.
- The Government MM ETF primarily holds short-term government securities.
- The Prime MM ETF covers a broader range including corporate commercial paper and CDs.
- Both are actively managed within strict regulatory guidelines.
- ETF wrapper lets investors trade money-market exposure on exchange with intraday liquidity.
- Karen Veraperry is iShares' Head of US Fixed Income Strategy.
