# The Ask: How are Americans investing in 2025?

## Summary

Samantha Ricci shares findings from BlackRock's People and Money survey (5,000+ US adults). 24 million Americans already own ETFs and 19 million more are very likely to buy in the next 12 months — 44% would be first-time ETF investors. Of those first-timers, 50%+ plan equity ETFs and 47% plan crypto ETPs. First-time investors trend younger and under $100K income, suggesting ETFs are removing barriers to entry.

## Key Takeaways

- Investing has surged in the US, with ETFs growing fast among new investors.
- BlackRock estimates 24M Americans already own ETFs.
- 19M more US adults are very likely to buy ETFs in the next 12 months.
- 44% of those will be first-time ETF investors — many under 35 and under $100K income.
- 50%+ of first-timers plan equity ETFs; 47% plan crypto ETPs.
- ETF growth reflects lower barriers to entry — younger, lower-income participation is rising.
