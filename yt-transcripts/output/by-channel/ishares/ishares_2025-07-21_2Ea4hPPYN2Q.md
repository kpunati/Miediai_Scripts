## Key Takeaways

- Income investing aims to generate a steady income stream from your portfolio.
- Three main paths: bonds, dividend-paying stocks, or actively managed income strategies.
- HDV targets high-dividend-paying or dividend-growing stocks (passive index).
- BALI is an active large-cap income ETF that uses call-option overlays for additional yield.

- BINC is an active fixed-income ETF managed by BlackRock's global CIO for fixed income, Rick Rieder.
