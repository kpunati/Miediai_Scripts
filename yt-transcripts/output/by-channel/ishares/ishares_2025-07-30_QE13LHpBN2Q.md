## Summary

Anna Day outlines how iShares ETFs support the retirement journey from accumulation through decumulation. Stresses two universal truths: it's never too late to start (but earlier is better) and retirement plans must last through retirement, not just to it. Highlights three product families: LifePath target-date ETFs (auto-rebalancing toward the retirement year), outcome ETFs (navigating volatility), and iBonds ETFs (defined-maturity bonds for managing retirement spending).

## Key Takeaways

- It's never too late to start retirement planning — but starting earlier compounds longer.
- Retirement plans must last through retirement, not just to it.
- LifePath target-date ETFs automatically rebalance global stocks and bonds as the target date approaches.
- Outcome ETFs help navigate volatile markets during accumulation or near retirement.
- iBonds ETFs (defined-maturity bonds) help retirees plan for spending needs by maturity date.
