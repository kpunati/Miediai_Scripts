# Vanguard Predicts It Won't Be As Good Moving Forward

**Channel:** Streamline Financial
**Published:** 2024-07-07
**URL:** https://youtube.com/watch?v=6z8oyJye0bo
**Duration:** 8:34

## Summary

Dave Zoller breaks down Vanguard's decade outlook (~2024-2034): US bonds projected to return 4.8-5.8% nominal annualized (much higher than pre-rate-hikes); international bonds 4.7-5.7%. 60/40 portfolio's probability of hitting 7%+ annualized increased. Vanguard sees US equities (especially large-cap growth) overvalued, with emerging markets and small-cap potentially undervalued. Cautions that Vanguard's 2014 decade forecast called for 5-8% global equity returns — actual was ~12%/yr.

## Key Takeaways

- Vanguard projects US bonds at 4.8-5.8% nominal annualized for the next decade — bonds are back.
- International bonds: 4.7-5.7% annualized projected.
- 60/40 portfolio's probability of hitting 7%+ annualized has improved per Vanguard.
- Higher interest rates expected to stay (not return to the 2-3% mortgage era).
- Vanguard sees US equities overvalued (especially large-cap growth); EM and small-cap as potentially undervalued.
- High-yield bonds may be overvalued — they often behave like stocks rather than bonds.
- Vanguard's 2014 forecast was wrong (predicted 5-8%, actual ~12%/yr) — plan around your needs, not forecasts.

## Detailed Notes

### Bonds are back per Vanguard [00:00:00]

Vanguard's outlook: US bonds 4.8-5.8% nominal annualized over the next decade — substantially higher than pre-rate-hike projections. International bonds 4.7-5.7%. 60/40 portfolio probability of hitting 7%+ annualized has improved.

### Higher interest rates here to stay [00:01:03]

Vanguard's view: even after policy rates recede from cyclical peaks, rates will settle higher than the 2010s norm. No return to 2-3% mortgages.

### Rates as positive: savings actually pay [00:02:07]

5% on safe money is real return after 14-15 years of near-zero. But watch inflation — the FED funds rate is back to par with CPI, so real yields are modest.

### Stock outlook: US overvalued, EM/small-cap undervalued [00:03:10]

Vanguard sees US equities most overvalued (especially large-cap growth). Emerging markets and small-cap may be undervalued. Portfolio reviews often show under-allocation to those.

### High-yield bonds behaving like stocks [00:04:14]

Vanguard flags high-yield as potentially overvalued. People often stretch for yield with high-yield credits — but they often behave like equity in drawdowns. A surprise in 2022 for many retirees.

### Forecasts: take with a grain of salt [00:05:18]

Vanguard and other economists make decade forecasts — but they've been wrong before. Dave urges focusing on YOUR plan and needs, not on whose decade forecast was right.

### Vanguard's wrong 2014 forecast [00:06:24]

Vanguard's 2014 outlook for 2014-2024 predicted 5-8% global equity returns with 50% probability of falling below 5% real. Actual decade was ~12%/yr. Forecasts inform but shouldn't drive plans.

### What to actually do [00:07:28]

Build your plan based on what you NEED and want, plus weighty historical evidence. Use what-if scenarios across rate, inflation, and return ranges to build resilient confidence — that lets you live the retirement you're looking forward to.
